About the Event

Data Science Talent Day is UC San Diego's annual recruiting event specifically dedicated to data science talent at UC San Diego. The two day event is hosted by the Data Science Student Society (DS3) in partnership with the Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute, and provides employers with a unique opportunity to meet, network, and recruit hundreds of our top-quality aspiring data scientists - from the largest data science organization in Califormia - who are seeking internships and job opportunities.
